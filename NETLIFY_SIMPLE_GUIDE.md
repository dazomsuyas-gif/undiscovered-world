## 🚀 **NETLIFY - COMPLETE WALKTHROUGH**

### **PART 1: Create Account**

1. Open browser
2. Go to: **https://netlify.com**
3. Click: **Sign up**
4. Choose:
   - **GitHub** (easiest)
   - **Google**
   - **Email**
5. Follow prompts
6. Verify email if needed

### **PART 2: Deploy Website**

1. After signing in, click: **Add new site**
2. Or click: **New site from Git** (if you see it)
3. Look for: **Drag files here to upload**

### **PART 3: Upload Files**

**Easiest way:**

1. Open folder: `C:\Users\Kelvin\Documents\GitHub\Undescovered-world-website`
2. Select all files (Ctrl+A)
3. Drag into Netlify window
4. Release mouse

**Done!**

### **PART 4: Wait for Deployment**

Netlify processes your files (usually 10-30 seconds).

You'll see: **Deployed!** ✅

### **PART 5: Get Your URL**

At the top, you'll see:

```
Your site is live at:
https://something-123.netlify.app
```

**This is your live website!**
