## 💬 **RESPONDING TO COMMENTS**

**Always reply to comments within 24 hours!**

Examples:

**Question:** "How much do you charge for eggs?"
**Reply:** "Great question! 🥚 We have fresh eggs at 500 TZS per dozen, 3,000 TZS per tray. Free delivery in Arusha for orders over 5,000 TZS. DM us or visit our website (link in bio) for more info! 😊"

**Compliment:** "Great content!"
**Reply:** "Thank you so much! 🙌 We love sharing farming tips! Follow for more daily advice. 🌾"

**Negative:** "Too expensive!"
**Reply:** "We understand! Our prices reflect quality, health certification, and sustainable practices. 💪 But we also offer bulk discounts! DM us to discuss options that fit your budget. 😊"
