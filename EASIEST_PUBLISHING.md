## 📋 **NETLIFY - STEP BY STEP**

### **Step 1: Go to Netlify**

Open browser → **https://netlify.com**

### **Step 2: Sign Up**

Click: **Sign up**

**Easiest: Click "GitHub"** (sign in with your GitHub account)

Or use email.

### **Step 3: Create New Site**

Click: **Add new site** or **Deploy**

### **Step 4: Upload Your Files**

You'll see: **Drag files here to upload**

**Do one of these:**

**Option A: Drag and drop entire folder**
1. Open: `C:\Users\Kelvin\Documents\GitHub\Undescovered-world-website`
2. Select all files
3. Drag into Netlify
4. **Done!**

**Option B: Click to select files**
1. Click upload area
2. Select files:
   - index.html
   - about.html
   - contact.html
   - styles.css
   - main.js
3. Upload

### **Step 5: Wait 10 seconds**

Netlify processes your files.

### **Step 6: Get Your URL**

You'll see:
```
Your site is live at:
https://random-name.netlify.app
```

**This is your website!**
